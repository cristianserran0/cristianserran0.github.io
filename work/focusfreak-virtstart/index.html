Virtstart is a virtual assistant platform by the startup FocusFreak. I have worked with the client since the service conception. I was in charge of the usability, user flows, mockups and low, mid and high fidelity prototypes. I designed the landing page and the UI of the webapp.

Eventually I ended up leading the rest of the project, supervising the webapp development, craft of an MVP and beta testing with real users.
